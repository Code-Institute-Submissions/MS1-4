Our Story

Inspired by authentic flavours, influenced by cultural cuisine,
and made with local ingredients.

Welcome to Blue Note Restaurant in the heart of Tralee, Co.Kerry. We serve breakfast, lunch and dinner Tuesday to Sunday. The warm and laid back atmosphere gives way to an exciting buzz as the day progresses. Our dedicated teams ensures every experience in Blue Note is one to remember.

Our passion lies is delivering organic produce cooked in a traditional way, with a contemporary flare. Our Irish menu with different cultural influences consists of organic fruits, vegetables and herb. All of our meats, fish and dairy are sourced locally, ensuring everything on our menu is fresh and utterly delicious. Each meal has passion poured into it, with no fuss or formality, it is simply a brilliant experience.

Our Chef

Brian Johnston was born in Co. Kerry where he now lives with his wife. He studied at Dublin’s Culinary College. He then went on to study in Paris, under Head Chef Pierre Roux of Michelin starred restaurant La Maison. On returning to Ireland, he became Head Chef of Rustic in Dublin.

In 2018 he opened The Blue Note Restaurant to follow his passion for organic produce and explore a new way of cooking. He has achieved great success combining his previous experience without the formality he was previously associated with. Brian grows the majority of the fruits, vegetables and herbs here in Tralee. His dedication sees him in the fields in the morning and going straight to the kitchen adapting the menu to produce that is fresh and in season. Brian strives to keep his ethos on organic and flavourful food.

Our Food

At The Blue Note Restaurant we are passionate about local ingredients. Head Chef and owner, Brian Johnston’s love for organic food, lead him to growing his own produce to meet the restaurants demands. The Blue Note Restaurant became certified by the Irish Organic Association in August 2018.

The fruits and vegetables are grown on our own certified garden which is run using no chemical sprays or fertilisers. The way the growth has evolved is to ensure ground nutrients and produce great quality seasonal produce. We work with local certified suppliers for our meats, fish and dairy. Celebrating Irish organic food at its best.
